Television Energy Usage

How Televisions Consume Energy

Televisions consume energy primarily through their display technology and internal components. LED and OLED screens are generally more energy-efficient than older LCD or plasma technologies.

Energy consumption can be affected by screen size, display technology, brightness settings, and how long the television remains on or in standby mode.

This page discusses energy consumption of televisions.

Ways to Save Energy

Choosing energy-efficient televisions, lowering screen brightness, and switching the TV off completely when not in use can help reduce electricity consumption.
